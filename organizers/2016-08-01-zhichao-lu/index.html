I’m an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Michigan State University. My research is focused on the intersection of Computer Vision, Biometrics, and Machine Learning. Specifically, I build computational models for analyzing human attributes, behaviors, and interactions from data.

I received my bachelor’s degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, and my Ph.D. from Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Vijayakumar Bhgavatula. Following my Ph.D., I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University where I worked with Takeo Kanade.
